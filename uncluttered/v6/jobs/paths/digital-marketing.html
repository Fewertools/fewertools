Digital Marketing Path

SEO, content, ads, analytics. The tools that get digital marketers hired, and promoted.

Your Path to Hired

1
Analytics Foundation

Google Analytics 4

Free

Every marketing job requires GA4. Set it up on a real site. Learn events, conversions, funnels. This is non-negotiable.

2
SEO

Google Search Console + Ahrefs Webmaster

Free

Understand organic traffic. Learn keyword research, on-page SEO, technical SEO basics. These free tools give you 80% of what paid tools offer.

3
Content Creation

Canva + ChatGPT

Free tiers

Canva for graphics and social posts. ChatGPT for drafting copy and brainstorming campaigns. The content creation powerhouse duo.

4
Email Marketing

Beehiiv

Free tier

Start a real newsletter. Shows you understand email funnels, open rates, CTR. Nothing proves marketing skills like actual subscriber numbers.

5
Social Scheduling

Buffer

Free tier

Plan and schedule posts across platforms. Learn to analyze what performs and why. Consistency is the #1 social media skill.

6
Ads Basics

Google Ads + Meta Ads

Free courses

You don't need to spend money. Understand the platforms, targeting, and metrics. Google's free certification alone is a resume booster.

7
Portfolio

Framer

Free tier

Build a beautiful site showcasing campaigns, results, and case studies. No code needed, just drag, drop, and publish.

What Hiring Managers Actually Look For

🎯

Run a REAL campaign

Even for a side project or fake brand. Hands-on experience with real data beats any certification. Create a brand, run ads, track results.

📊

Numbers talk

"Grew newsletter to 500 subscribers in 3 months" beats "experienced in email marketing". Quantify everything on your resume.

📜

Get Google Analytics certified

It's completely free and takes a weekend. Nearly every marketing job listing mentions it. Low effort, high signal.

✍️

Learn to write

It's the most underrated marketing skill. Good copy drives conversions, engagement, and SEO. Marketers who write well get promoted faster.
