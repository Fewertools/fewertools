Finance & Accounting Path

Spreadsheets, reporting, compliance. The tools that get finance professionals hired.

Your Path

1
Spreadsheets mastery

Excel + Google Sheets

Free

Advanced formulas, pivot tables, financial models. This is still the #1 skill in finance. Master VLOOKUP, INDEX/MATCH, and pivot tables before anything else.

2
Accounting software

Xero / QuickBooks

Free trial / Courses

Most SMBs use Xero (UK) or QuickBooks (US). Understanding these is expected. Learn invoicing, reconciliation, and reporting through free trials or online courses.

3
Data visualization

Power BI / Tableau Public

Free desktop

Turn financial data into dashboards. CFOs love analysts who can visualize. Build dashboards that tell the story behind the numbers, not just display them.

4
Financial modelling

Excel + Google Sheets

Free

DCF models, budgets, forecasting. Build 2–3 sample models for your portfolio. A well-structured financial model in an interview separates you from the pack.

5
AI assistant

ChatGPT / Claude

Free tiers

Draft reports, explain regulations, create templates, analyze trends. AI won't replace finance professionals, but finance professionals using AI will replace those who don't.

6
Documentation

Notion

Free

Organize processes, compliance checklists, audit trails. Being the person who documents everything properly is how you become indispensable in a finance team.

7
Certification prep

ACCA/CIMA (UK) or CPA (US)

Paid qualifications

Professional qualifications massively boost earning potential in finance. Start studying early, even being "part-qualified" opens doors that experience alone can't.

What Hiring Managers Actually Look For

📊

Excel is tested in interviews

Practice under pressure. Many finance interviews include a live Excel test, timed spreadsheet exercises, model building, or data analysis. Practise with real datasets and a timer running.

📑

Know your financial statements

Get comfortable with the P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow statement. Understand how they connect. This is the bare minimum, if you can't walk through these fluently, you won't get past round one.

🎓

Qualifications open senior doors

ACCA/ACA/CIMA (UK) or CPA (US) are the golden tickets. Even being "part-qualified" significantly boosts your CV. Start early, the ROI on these certifications is enormous.

💡

Numbers AND the business story

Show you understand both the numbers and what they mean for the business. Anyone can read a spreadsheet, the best finance hires explain what the data means and what to do about it.

💰

Average Salary Range

£28–55K UK  /  $55–95K US

Mid-level finance & accounting professional. Senior roles, qualified accountants, and FP&A specialists earn significantly more. ACA/ACCA/CPA qualification typically adds 20–40% to your salary.
