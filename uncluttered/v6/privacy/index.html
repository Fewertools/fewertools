Privacy Policy

We believe in minimal data collection. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

Last updated: February 2026

The short version: We collect minimal data, don't sell anything to third parties, and respect your privacy. If you just browse the site, we collect basic analytics. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email.

Who We Are

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at clinton@fewertools.com.

What We Collect

Analytics Data

We use Vercel Analytics and Speed Insights to understand how visitors use our site. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring websites
  • Browser type and device
  • Country-level location (not precise)

This data is aggregated and anonymized. We cannot identify individual users from analytics.

Newsletter Subscription

If you subscribe to our newsletter (powered by Beehiiv), we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Subscription date and source
  • Email open and click activity

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

Contact Form

If you use our contact form (powered by Formspree), we receive:

  • Your name and email
  • The message you send

We use this only to respond to your inquiry.

What We Don't Collect

  • We don't use cookies for tracking or advertising
  • We don't collect payment information (we don't sell anything directly)
  • We don't track you across other websites
  • We don't build profiles about you

Third-Party Services

We use these services that may process your data:

  • Vercel - Hosting and analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Beehiiv - Newsletter platform (Privacy Policy)
  • Formspree - Contact forms (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts - Typography (Privacy Policy)

Affiliate Links

Some links on our site are affiliate links. When you click them and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. The affiliate programs we participate in may use cookies to track referrals. See our Affiliate Disclosure for details.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of data we have about you
  • Deletion - Request we delete your data
  • Correction - Request we update incorrect data
  • Unsubscribe - Opt out of our newsletter anytime

To exercise these rights, email clinton@fewertools.com.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Our site is served over HTTPS, and our third-party providers maintain their own security practices.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted on this page with an updated date.

Questions?
