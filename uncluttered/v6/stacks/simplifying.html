Simplifying

For cutting what doesn't serve you

You have too many tools. Too many subscriptions. Too much complexity. This isn't about adding something new ‚Äî it's about removing what no longer belongs.

You're in the right place if:

  • You're paying for tools you barely use
  • Context-switching between apps is killing your focus
  • You can't remember what tool does what anymore
  • Onboarding new team members takes forever
  • Your monthly SaaS bill makes you uncomfortable

The Audit

Before you cut, understand what you have and why.

List Everything

Every tool, every subscription, every integration. You can't simplify what you can't see.

  • Check your credit card statements
  • Review team app usage
  • Document integrations

Track Usage

For two weeks, note which tools you actually use daily vs. which just sit there.

  • Daily active use?
  • Weekly? Monthly?
  • Last login date

Find Overlaps

Multiple tools doing the same job? That's where complexity hides.

  • Two note-taking apps?
  • Multiple analytics tools?
  • Redundant communication
