Growing

For scaling what works

You have users. Real ones. Now you need to understand their behavior, catch issues before they do, and build systems that scale. Different problems, different tools.

You're in the right place if:

  • You have paying customers or active users
  • You need to understand user behavior, not just ship features
  • Bug reports are coming in faster than you can fix them
  • You're thinking about team processes and workflows
  • Support requests need a better system

Get started
