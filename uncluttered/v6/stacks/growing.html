Growing

For scaling what works

People are using your product. Revenue is coming in. Now you need to understand your users better, communicate at scale, and not break what's already working.

You're in the right place if:

  • You have paying customers or active users
  • You need to understand user behavior, not just ship features
  • Support requests are becoming hard to manage manually
  • You're thinking about retention, not just acquisition
  • Your to-do list needs structure beyond a notes app

Your Stack

Four tools to understand, communicate, and scale.

PostHog Analytics

Product analytics that actually tells you what users do. Session recordings, funnels, feature flags. All in one tool, self-hostable if you care about data privacy.

Why this, not that

Not Google Analytics (traffic, not product analytics). Not Mixpanel (expensive at scale). Not Amplitude (enterprise complexity). PostHog gives you everything without the vendor lock-in.

Loops Email

Email marketing built for SaaS. Onboarding sequences, product updates, re-engagement campaigns. Simple, beautiful, designed for product teams.

Why this, not that

Not Mailchimp (bloated, not product-focused). Not ConvertKit (for creators, not SaaS). Not Customer.io (enterprise pricing). Loops is email for products, not newsletters.

Intercom Support

Talk to users at scale. Live chat, help center, automated responses. When email threads and Slack DMs stop working, Intercom takes over.

Why this, not that

Not Zendesk (enterprise complexity). Not Crisp (limited automation). Not plain email (doesn't scale). Intercom is expensive but worth it when support becomes a bottleneck.

Get started

Linear Project Management

Issue tracking that doesn't make you hate issue tracking. Fast, keyboard-driven, opinionated. When your roadmap needs more structure than Notion.

Why this, not that

Not Jira (soul-crushing). Not Asana (too generic). Not Notion (not built for this). Linear is project management for people who actually build products.
