Starting

For exploring and validating ideas

You have an idea ‚Äî maybe several. Before you build anything, you need to explore, validate, and think clearly. These tools help you do that without getting in the way.

You're in the right place if:

  • You're exploring an idea but haven't started building
  • You need to organize thoughts and do research
  • You want to validate before you invest time coding
  • You're sketching interfaces and user flows
  • You're talking to potential users or customers
