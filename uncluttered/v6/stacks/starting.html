Starting

For exploring and validating

You have an idea. Maybe several. You're figuring out what's possible, what's viable, what's worth building. You need tools that help you think and share ‚Äî not tools that help you scale.

You're in the right place if:

  • You have an idea but haven't written code yet
  • You're validating whether this is worth building
  • You're talking to potential users or customers
  • You're still figuring out the core problem
  • You need to organize thoughts, not organize teams

Your Stack

Five tools. Everything you need to go from idea to decision.

Notion Home Base

Your second brain. Ideas, notes, research, planning ‚Äî it all lives here. Flexible enough to be whatever you need it to be right now.

Why this, not that

Not Obsidian (you're not building a knowledge graph). Not Google Docs (too scattered). Not Linear (you don't have a team yet). Notion is malleable ‚Äî it grows with your thinking.

Figma Design

Make your ideas visible. Wireframes, mockups, landing page drafts. Show people what you're thinking instead of explaining it.

Why this, not that

Not Sketch (Mac-only, less collaborative). Not Adobe XD (dying). Not Canva (too template-heavy for product thinking). Figma is where ideas become real before you write code.

Get started
üìä
Google Sheets Data

Track what matters. User research responses, competitor analysis, simple financial models. Free, shareable, surprisingly powerful.

Why this, not that

Not Airtable (overkill for validation). Not Excel (sharing friction). Not a database (you don't need one yet). Sheets is the right amount of structure for unstructured exploration.

Get started
üí¨
Slack Communication

Talk to your first users. Create a community channel, gather feedback, build in public. The conversations here will shape what you build.

Why this, not that

Not Discord (better for communities, but Slack is better for early product feedback). Not email (too slow, too formal). Slack creates the tight feedback loop you need right now.

Get started
üß†
Claude AI Thinking Partner

Your brainstorming partner. Validate ideas, research competitors, write landing page copy, think through problems. Like having a smart cofounder who's always available.

Why this, not that

Not ChatGPT (Claude reasons better on complex decisions). Not Perplexity (great for research, but Claude is better for thinking through problems with you). Claude is for depth, not just answers.
