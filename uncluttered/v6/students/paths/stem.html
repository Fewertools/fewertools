💡 Pro Tips

  • Learn Python early, it's used in every STEM field, from biology to aerospace engineering.
  • Google Colab gives you free GPU computing. Perfect for machine learning coursework without buying hardware.
  • Always back up lab data in multiple places. Cloud + local. Data loss = wasted months.
  • LaTeX skills impress professors and look great on CVs. Start with Overleaf templates, don't write from scratch.
  • Use Zotero from day one. Manually formatting a bibliography at 2am before a deadline is pain you can avoid.
