Anki Free

Flashcards for statutes, case names, legal principles. Spaced repetition is essential for law, you'll retain far more than re-reading notes ever achieves.

5

Notion + ChatGPT Free

Outline problem questions, map out IRAC structure, identify relevant authorities. Use Notion for the plan, ChatGPT to challenge your reasoning.

⚖️ Pro Tips

  • Brief every case as you read it, future you will be grateful when exams arrive.
  • IRAC structure is your best friend. Issue, Rule, Application, Conclusion. Drill it until it's instinct.
  • Start Anki from week 1. Don't wait until revision period, the whole point is spaced repetition over time.
  • Read the judgment, not just the textbook summary. You'll understand the reasoning, not just the outcome.
  • Build a personal case law database in Notion. Tag by topic, principle, and module. It compounds over three years.
