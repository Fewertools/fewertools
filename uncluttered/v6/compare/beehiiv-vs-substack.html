Beehiiv vs Substack

Two newsletter platforms for creators. One gives you growth tools, the other gives you a built-in audience. Here's when each wins.

⚡ Our Verdict

Beehiiv wins for serious newsletter operators.

If you're treating your newsletter as a business, Beehiiv's growth tools, referral programs, ad network, and 0% revenue cut make it the clear choice. Substack is great for writers who want simplicity and a built-in discovery network.

When to Use Each

🟢 Use Beehiiv when…

  • You're building a newsletter as a business
  • You want referral programs and growth tools
  • You plan to monetize with ads + subscriptions
  • You need advanced segmentation and automations
  • You don't want to give up 10% of revenue

🔵 Use Substack when…

  • You're a writer focused on content, not operations
  • You want built-in discovery from the Substack network
  • You prefer simplicity over features
  • You're just starting and want zero upfront costs
  • You want podcast and community features built in

🎯 Our Recommendation

For creators serious about growing a newsletter business, Beehiiv is the clear winner. The referral system alone can 10x your organic growth, and keeping 100% of your revenue (vs losing 10% to Substack) adds up fast. Substack is still excellent if you're a writer who values simplicity and wants to tap into their discovery network, but the moment you start thinking about scale, Beehiiv's tools are in a different league.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Substack in 2026?

For growth-focused creators, yes. Beehiiv offers referral programs, audience segmentation, A/B testing, and a built-in ad network, tools Substack simply doesn't have. However, Substack has a stronger built-in discovery network and a simpler, more writer-friendly experience.

Does Substack take a cut of paid subscriptions?

Yes. Substack takes 10% of all paid subscription revenue, plus Stripe processing fees (~3%). Beehiiv charges 0% on its paid plans, you only pay your monthly platform fee and Stripe fees. At scale, this difference is significant.

Can I migrate from Substack to Beehiiv?

Yes. Beehiiv has a one-click Substack importer that brings over your subscribers, posts, and metadata. The migration is straightforward and typically takes minutes. Many large newsletters have successfully migrated.

Does Beehiiv have a free plan?

Yes. Beehiiv's Launch plan is free for up to 2,500 subscribers and includes unlimited sends, a custom website, and basic analytics. It's a solid way to start without any upfront investment. Paid plans unlock growth tools and advanced features.

Which platform is better for monetization?

Beehiiv, by far. It offers multiple monetization paths: paid subscriptions (0% platform cut), a built-in ad network, Boosts (get paid to recommend other newsletters), and premium content gating. Substack only offers paid subscriptions with a 10% cut.
