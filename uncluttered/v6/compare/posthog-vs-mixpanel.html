Frequently Asked Questions

Is PostHog really free?

PostHog's free tier is extremely generous: 1 million events/month for analytics, 5,000 session recordings, 1 million feature flag evaluations, and 250 survey responses. For most early-stage products, you won't pay anything for months. Paid usage is pay-as-you-go.

Can PostHog replace Mixpanel, Hotjar, and LaunchDarkly?

Yes ‚Äî that's the pitch. PostHog combines product analytics (Mixpanel), session replays (Hotjar), feature flags (LaunchDarkly), A/B testing, and surveys in one platform. Each individual feature isn't always as deep as the dedicated tool, but the integration between them is powerful.

Is Mixpanel easier to use than PostHog?

For non-technical team members, yes. Mixpanel's UI is more polished and intuitive for building funnels, cohorts, and reports. PostHog is developer-focused ‚Äî it's powerful but assumes comfort with technical concepts. If your product/marketing team needs self-serve analytics, Mixpanel has a better learning curve.

Can I self-host PostHog?

Yes. PostHog is fully open-source and can be self-hosted on your own infrastructure. This gives you complete data ownership and avoids sending user data to third parties. Mixpanel is cloud-only with no self-hosting option.

Which has better event tracking?

PostHog has autocapture ‚Äî it automatically tracks clicks, pageviews, and form submissions without manual instrumentation. Mixpanel requires explicit event tracking code. PostHog gets you started faster; Mixpanel's manual approach gives cleaner, more intentional data.
