FAQ

Common questions

What platform do you build on?

+
It depends on the project. Most sites are custom HTML/CSS/JS for maximum speed, or Next.js for more complex builds. I'll recommend the best approach based on what you need, not what's trendy.

Do you do hosting and domains?

+
Yes. I'll set up hosting (usually Vercel or Netlify, both free for most sites) and help you get your domain connected. No ongoing hosting fees in most cases.

How long does a project take?

+
Starter sites take about 5-7 days. Professional builds are 10-14 days. Premium projects take 2-3 weeks. These timelines assume timely feedback on designs.

What if I need changes after launch?

+
Small tweaks after launch are included for 30 days. For ongoing support or larger changes, we can set up a monthly retainer that works for both of us.

Can I update the site myself?

+
If you need to update content regularly, I'll set up a CMS (like a blog or portfolio manager) so you can make changes without touching code. Otherwise, you get the full source code and can hire anyone to make changes later.

What do you need from me to get started?

+
Just a rough idea of what you want: your business/project name, what the site should do, any sites you like the look of, and your content (or I can help write it). We'll figure the rest out together.
