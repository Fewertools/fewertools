Overview

ConvertKit is the email platform of choice for creators, bloggers, podcasters, and course sellers. Unlike Mailchimp's one-size-fits-all approach, ConvertKit is laser-focused on helping creators build and monetize their audience.

The platform shines with its visual automation builder, subscriber tagging system, and built-in commerce features. You can sell digital products, run paid newsletters, and create sophisticated email sequences—all without needing a separate tool.
