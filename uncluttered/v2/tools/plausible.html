Overview

Plausible is the analytics tool for people who are tired of Google Analytics. It's simple, fast, and respects your visitors' privacy. The entire dashboard fits on one page—no training needed to understand your traffic.

Because Plausible doesn't use cookies or collect personal data, you don't need cookie consent banners. This makes it GDPR, CCPA, and PECR compliant out of the box. Plus, the script is under 1KB, so it won't slow down your site.
