## Hook Formulas

Hooks that work for finance content:

1. **Contrarian:** "Everyone's bullish on X. Here's what they're missing."
2. **Breakdown:** "I analyzed [STOCK] in 60 seconds. Here's the score."
3. **Numbers:** "[STOCK]'s G.R.O.W. score just dropped 15 points."
4. **Question:** "What makes NVDA worth 30x earnings? Let's break it down."
5. **Comparison:** "[STOCK A] vs [STOCK B] ‚Äî G.R.O.W. score comparison."

## What Not To Tweet

- Your personal trades
- Price predictions
- "Buy now" urgency
- Defensive "not financial advice" disclaimers
- Engagement bait unrelated to investing
- Politics, controversy, hot takes outside your lane

## Growth Tactics

### Week 1-4: Foundation
- Post consistently (7-10 tweets/week)
- Follow 50-100 investing accounts
- Reply to conversations with value-add comments
- Don't pitch product in replies

### Week 5-8: Expansion
- Guest threads on other accounts
- Quote-tweet interesting analysis with G.R.O.W. perspective
- Start tagging stocks ($NVDA format for discoverability)
- Engage in Spaces (listen, comment, don't pitch)

### Week 9-12: Acceleration
- Cross-promote with other small accounts
- Consider paid promotion on best threads
- Reddit/HN cross-posting of analysis
- Email list building via lead magnet
