## Annual Review Questions

Ask yourself at year end:
1. Did I beat the S&P 500? If not, why am I stock picking?
2. Which positions outperformed? What did they have in common?
3. Which positions underperformed? What patterns do I see?
4. Did I stick to my system or deviate emotionally?
5. What's my biggest learning this year?
6. What will I do differently next year?

---

*Track consistently. Review honestly. Improve continuously.*
